Creamy Chicken Alfredo

A Rich, Comforting Pasta Dish with Tender Chicken and a Silky Parmesan Sauce That’s Perfect for Family Dinners or a Cozy Night

Creamy Chicken Alfredo is a classic Italian-American pasta dish that combines tender chicken with smooth, rich Alfredo sauce. The sauce is made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a luxurious coating for perfectly cooked fettuccine noodles. Each bite is warm, creamy, and deeply satisfying, making it a favorite for comfort food lovers.

This dish is not only flavorful but also easy to prepare, which makes it ideal for busy weeknights or special occasions. Served with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or extra Parmesan, it’s a meal that feels indulgent while still being simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (fettuccine or similar)
  • Chicken breast
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until tender, then drain and set aside.
  2. Cook chicken until golden and fully cooked, then slice.
  3. Melt butter in a pan, sauté garlic, and add cream to simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan until smooth, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until coated.
  6. Top with sliced chicken and mix gently.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy.
